Welcome to BattleShip!

Please choose where you would like to place your 3 Squares.

The Objective: To Win this game you have to sink the computer squares before they sink all yours. Rules: You each will take turns trying to hit each others square. If you guess all 3 blocks before your opponent then you Win! You will go back and forth until there is only one person standing. After placing your squares click 'Play' and let the adventure begin, Good luck on your voyage!
